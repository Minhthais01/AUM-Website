About Us

Welcome to AUM (Annual University Magazine)!

We are a hub for sharing, organizing, and managing articles efficiently. At AUM, we understand that managing and accessing textual resources is an essential part of research, learning, and work. With that mission in mind, we've built a multifunctional platform to help you organize and manage articles smartly and easily.

At AUM, you'll discover useful features such as:

  • Efficient organization: Sort articles by categories, labels, and tags for easy search and access.
  • Convenient sharing: Share articles with colleagues, friends, or partners easily and quickly.
  • Notes and comments: Create notes and comments directly on articles to capture your thoughts and ideas.
  • Cross-platform integration: Use across multiple devices and different browsers, from desktop to mobile.

We are committed to providing the best user experience and always listen to feedback from the community. Let's create a vibrant community and grow together with AUM!
